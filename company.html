Legal Battle Update

As many of you know we are currently in a legal battle with Towel Town. They claim that we have been stealing our ideas. We have come up with our own ideas and they do not resemble the same designs that Towel Town creates for their towels. We had a court hearing yesterday and we are optimistic that we will win the case. If we were to lose the case, we would not be forced to shut down, we would need to stop producing some of your favorite laptop cover designs though.

Next Project

We are in the final stages of our newest desktop case. The hydro blaster. This is a water cooling case with a thermometer on it to let you know how cool the computer is.
